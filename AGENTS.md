Background  
- Consulting service providing easily updatable websites/landing pages.  
- Emphasis on client control, no vendor lock-in, optional use of agents/tools.

Website Purpose & Positioning  
- Marketing/lead-generation for consulting around automated, easily updatable sites and workflows.  
- Focus on automation, workflows, conversions, and analytics.  
- Messaging centered on customer problems and outcomes.  
- Agents/tools framed as optional enablers; core AI agent for content updates.  
- Timelines positioned as fast (hours for simple pages; weeks+ for complex).  
- Future-facing automation positioning with concise, punchy tagline.

Visual & UX Preferences  
- Dark, modern, polished “productized SaaS/agency” aesthetic.  
- Clean typography with strong hierarchy.  
- Blue and purple accents.  
- Preference for professionally styled numbered elements over icons/badges.  
- All section headings and subheadings centered.  
- Navbar primary CTA visually restrained on mobile.  
- No social media icons in footer.  
- Logo system: image logo plus gradient text logo; on scroll, mobile hides text logo and keeps image logo; desktop keeps both.  
- Logo text kept fully visible without being cut off.  
- Hero CTAs full-width on small mobile.

Hero & Layout Decisions  
- Single-page layout.  
- Hero main heading max size 3.6rem, two fixed lines: “Your Website” / “Our Problem”.  
- Hero heading gradient at 135°, weight 600, sequential entrance animation (top pops, bottom slides from left).  
- Hero layout fully centered; no separate right-side hero element; content fixed.  
- Hero checklist cycles three items one at a time in a continuous loop.  
- Hero primary CTA labeled “Get Started”; secondary CTA labeled “See Pricing”.

Pricing & Content Structure Decisions  
- Middle value proposition emphasizes automation/workflows, with optional agent references.  
- Single testimonial.  
- Three-tier pricing with mid-tier explicitly most popular.  
- Pricing shows original vs lower promotional price.  
- Top-tier primary price visually equal to mid-tier, not struck through.  
- Top-tier custom option labeled “Custom Pricing” and visually treated as a normal price.  
- Buttons within comparable cards aligned to bottom for consistent vertical alignment.

Technical & Navigation Constraints  
- Single HTML file including markup, styles, and scripts.  
- Contact form submits to external API endpoint.  
- Navbar contains only “Pricing” and “Let’s Go”.  
- Desktop: navbar links/CTA initially hidden, revealed with animated focus transition when navbar reaches hero CTA vertical position, with special reveal for “Pricing” and “Let’s Go”.  
- Mobile: navbar always shows “Pricing” and “Let’s Go”, no hamburger menu.  
- Footer privacy link points to dedicated privacy policy page.
